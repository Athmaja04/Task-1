Stroke Assistance

Recognize the signs of a stroke:

  1. Sudden numbness or weakness, especially on one side of the body
  2. Confusion, trouble speaking, or understanding speech
  3. Sudden trouble seeing in one or both eyes
  4. Sudden trouble walking, dizziness, or loss of balance
What to Do:

What to do if you suspect someone is having a stroke:

  1. Call emergency services immediately.
  2. Note the time when symptoms started to help medical personnel.
  3. Keep the person calm and seated while waiting for help to arrive.
