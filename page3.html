Electric Shock Assistance

Steps to Take During an Electric Shock:

  1. Turn off the source of electricity if it is safe to do so. Use a wooden or plastic object to move the source away from the person.
  2. Do NOT touch the person directly if they are still in contact with the electrical source.
  3. Once the person is free from the electrical source, check for responsiveness and breathing.
  4. If they are not breathing or have no pulse, start CPR immediately while waiting for emergency services.
  5. Cover burns with a sterile gauze or clean cloth but avoid applying ointments or creams.
  6. Stay with the person until medical help arrives.

Important Notes:

  • Always call emergency services immediately in case of an electric shock.
  • If the person has fallen from a height due to the shock, avoid moving them unless it is absolutely necessary for safety.
