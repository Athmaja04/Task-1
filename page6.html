Choking Assistance

Steps to Take if Someone is Choking:

  1. Encourage the person to cough if they are able to, as this can help dislodge the object.
  2. If the person is unable to cough or breathe, give them 5 back blows.
  3. Stand behind the person and use the heel of your hand to deliver 5 sharp blows between the shoulder blades.
  4. If the back blows don't work, perform abdominal thrust.
  5. Stand behind the person and perform quick inward and upward thrusts.
  6. Continue cycles of back blows and abdominal thrusts until the person begins breathing.
  7. If the person becomes unconscious, lower them to the ground and begin CPR.

Important Notes:

  • Do NOT perform abdominal thrusts on children under 1 year old. Instead, give 5 back blows and 5 chest thrusts.
  • Do NOT attempt to remove the object if the person is unconscious, as you may push it further down.
