Burns Assistance

Steps to Take for Burn Injuries:

  1. Immediately remove the person from the source of the burn if it is safe to do so.
  2. Cool the burn under cold running water for at least 10 minutes.
  3. If the burn is severe, avoid removing burned clothing, as this can cause further harm.
  4. Cover the burn with a sterile non-stick dressing or a clean cloth to prevent infection.
  5. Do NOT apply ice or ointments as these can make the injury worse.
  6. If the person is in pain, provide them with ibuprofen or acetaminophen.
  7. Seek medical attention immediately for any severe burns.
  8. If the burn covers a large area of the body.

Important Notes:

  • Do NOT break blisters, as this increases the risk of infection.
  • If the burn is caused by chemicals, flush the area with water.
