Snake Bite Assistance

Steps to Take in Case of a Snake Bite:

  1. Stay calm and try to identify the snake (if it's safe to do so).
  2. Call emergency services immediately and inform them about the snake bite.
  3. Keep the victim still and try to keep the affected limb immobilized at or slightly below the level of the heart.
  4. Do NOT try to suck out venom or cut the wound.
  5. Remove tight clothing or jewelry near the bite area as swelling can occur.
  6. If possible, take note of the snake's color, size, and markings to assist medical professionals.
  7. Monitor for signs of shock and keep the person warm until help arrives.

Important Notes:

  • Never attempt to capture or kill the snake unless you are absolutely certain it is safe.
  • Snake bites can sometimes be non-venomous, but still require immediate medical attention.
